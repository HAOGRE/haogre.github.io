<p>Little Bird — Modern People Band</p>

<p>I'm like a little bird flying here and there, soaring high,</p>
<p>until one day I flew to a lively, bustling place — oh</p>

<p>That dazzling, kaleidoscopic spinning stage, like a village in full bloom,</p>
<p>those cars and trams, trams and cars and bicycles, flowing like a river</p>
<p>Some people pretend they've seen through the red dust, staring at walls all day long,</p>
<p>and those girls who never seem to grow up, so innocent and generous — oh,</p>
<p>you see the patriotic fever in the football stadium, wave after wave rising higher</p>
<p>imported assembled imported original TV ads blaring ever louder</p>
<p>smoking foreign cigarettes drinking foreign wine eating foreign food, wearing brand names real and fake,</p>
<p>playing breakdance playing rock 'n' roll playing with trends</p>
<p>hey — just to make you feel very Western</p>
<p>learning foreign languages to raise your status, always talking with a Cantonese accent,</p>
<p>chin in hand, silent, gaze unwavering — also a new kind of style</p>
<p>coffee cola shampoo diet pills, all with big markets now,</p>
<p>red papers green papers staring at you from telephone poles, whispering centuries of imperial secrets</p>
<p>splash splash splash splash splash splash, everyone's free time is so busy,</p>
<p>karaoke halls and video parlors don't know what a day off is, singing all day long</p>
<p>fifteen- and sixteen-year-old boys and girls, young and beautiful,</p>
<p>with idols in their hearts, so many from Taiwan and Hong Kong,</p>
<p>take a look, look around — good role models are right beside you,</p>
<p>study hard and improve every day, keep your eyes on the big picture</p>
<p>I'm like a little bird flying here and there, soaring high,</p>
<p>until one day I flew to a lively, bustling place — oh</p>
<p>That dazzling, kaleidoscopic spinning stage, like a village in full bloom,</p>
<p>those cars and trams, trams and cars and bicycles, flowing like a river</p>
