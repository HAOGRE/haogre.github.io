After years of window-shopping, I finally pulled the trigger and bought a Gen10 home server. About a week of tinkering later, here's a quick write-up.

The original goal: take all the random bits of infrastructure at home and see if I could cram them into a single box. More or less achieved.

![The setup](/uploads/nrs.jpeg)

LEDE + NAS + CentOS + Win-Server all running in one small box — pretty satisfying. That's about it.

Down the road:

- If I can get a high-powered wireless card to use as an AP, I could retire the K3 router entirely
- If I can get a HBA card to expand to 8 drive bays, that would be the dream
