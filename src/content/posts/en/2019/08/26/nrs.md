<p>After years of window-shopping, I finally watched long enough — then bought a Gen10 home server and knocked that project off the list.</p>
<p>About a week of tinkering later, here's a quick write-up.</p>
<p>The original goal: take all the random bits of infrastructure at home and see if I could cram them into a single box. More or less achieved.</p>

<p><img src="/uploads/nrs.jpeg" alt="The setup"></p>
<p>LEDE + NAS + CentOS + Win-Server all running in one small box — pretty satisfying.</p>
<p>That's about it.</p>
<p>Down the road: if I can get a high-powered wireless card to use as an AP, I could retire the K3 router entirely.</p>
<p>Even better: if I can get a HBA card to expand to 8 drive bays, that would be the dream.</p>
